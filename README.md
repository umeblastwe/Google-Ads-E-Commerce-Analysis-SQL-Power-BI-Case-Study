# Google-Ads-E-Commerce-Analysis-SQL-Power-BI-Case-Study
Project Overview
This project analyzes a high-volume Google Ads dataset (over 750,000 rows) to identify customer behavior patterns, friction points in the sales funnel, and opportunities for revenue growth. By combining SQL for data transformation and Power BI for visualization, I uncovered actionable insights regarding device performance, geographic trends, and seasonal sales cycles.

🛠️ Tech Stack
Data Analysis: SQL Server (Complex CTEs, Case Statements, Data Aggregation)

Data Visualization: Power BI (DAX Measures, Funnel Charts, Time-Intelligence)

📊 Key Insights
1. The "Leaky" Funnel
Finding: We observed a massive drop-off between "Add to Cart" (667k) and "Begin Checkout" (76k).

Insight: 88% of high-intent users are leaving before starting the checkout process.

Recommendation: Simplify the transition from cart to checkout with "Guest Checkout" options.

2. Device Success Rates
Finding: Mobile leads in efficiency with a 21.34% checkout-to-purchase rate, beating Desktop (19.98%).

Insight: Our mobile checkout flow is highly optimized and trusted by users.

3. Geographic Performance
Finding: The United States is the primary revenue driver, with India and Canada following as top-tier markets.

4. Peak Performance Hours
Finding: Sales peak significantly at 10:00 PM and 8:00 PM.

Insight: Late-night "Night Owls" are the most active shoppers.

Recommendation: Shift ad budget toward 6 PM – 11 PM to maximize ROAS.

5. Seasonality
Finding: Sales peaked in December (6,775 purchases) but saw a sharp decline in January (2,590 purchases).

Insight: The business is highly seasonal and reliant on holiday spending.
